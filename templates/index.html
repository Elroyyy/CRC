About Us

Dehiwala Community Church is a Christ-centered community built on Worship, the Word, and Service. We exist to bring the light and life of Jesus Christ to our neighborhood and beyond, proclaiming the gospel in both word and action. We are associated with Christian Revival Crusade, and we joyfully embrace our identity as a charismatic church while remaining firmly grounded in the orthodox Christian faith. We value the work of the Holy Spirit, the authority of Scripture, and the historic truths of the Church.


At our core, we are a family-oriented church that believes faith is best lived out in community. We seek to nurture meaningful relationships, walk alongside one another in life’s journey, and intentionally build and disciple serious followers of Christ. Whether you are exploring faith or growing deeper in your walk with God, you are welcome here. Our mission is simple: to love God wholeheartedly, serve our community faithfully, and make disciples who reflect Christ in everyday life.

Our Story

Dehiwala Community Church traces its roots to a humble beginning in 1987, when Senior Pastor Navaradnarajah Somanathan and his wife Jeevardharshini Somanathan responded to God’s call and began the ministry under the name Streams of Power. What started as a small step of faith soon grew into a vibrant work, grounded in prayer, the Word of God, and a deep love for people. In the years that followed, the ministry became part of Christian Revival Crusade (CRC)—a Pentecostal denomination headquartered in Australia—embracing a wider vision while remaining committed to serving the local community. After many years of waiting, praying, and faithfully believing, the CRC headquarters and church space were finally completed in 2017, marking a significant milestone in the journey of the ministry.


Pastor Somanathan has faithfully served the Lord and His people for over 42 years, with Jeevardharshini standing alongside him as a steadfast support and gifted administrator of the ministry. Together, their servant leadership has helped nurture both spiritual growth and practical outreach. Over time, the ministry expanded to include two children’s homes in Valaichchenai, a pre-school in Vavuniya, and several church plants, reflecting a heart for holistic mission—reaching children, families, and communities with the love of Christ.


In 2025, the leadership sensed a renewed vision from God: to focus intentionally on the immediate neighborhood of Dehiwala. Out of this calling, Dehiwala Community Church was born—a local expression of the wider CRC family, committed to being a beacon of hope to the city of Dehiwala. Today, Dehiwala Community Church exists to worship God, proclaim His Word, and serve our community with compassion. We are grateful for the journey so far and look forward with faith to all that God will continue to do among us.
